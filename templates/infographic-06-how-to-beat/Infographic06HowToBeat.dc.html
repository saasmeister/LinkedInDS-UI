HOW TO BEAT:
“We don’t know how
to onboard users”
A repeatable way to turn one painful objection into a carousel that converts — steal the structure, swap in your topic.
THE SETUP
1. SCOPE
One flow, one outcome. Not the whole product.
2. LENGTH
5–7 steps. Enough to be useful, short enough to finish.
3. TOOLS
Figma, Notion, code — whatever your team already uses.
MAKE IT STICK
1. SIMPLIFY
One job per screen. Plain language. Show the next action.
2. RULE OF ONE
One user, one problem, one action. Write it as “How we…”
