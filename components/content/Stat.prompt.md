One big number with a caption — the hero figure on a single or result slide. `StatBox`/`StatRow` build the infographic results rail.

```jsx
<Stat value="3.4×" caption="more replies" />

<StatRow label="RESULTS">
  <StatBox value="10k" caption="followers" />
  <StatBox value="90"  caption="days" />
</StatRow>
```

- `Stat size="xl"` is the 230px hero treatment for a section result slide.
- `StatBox tone="section"` (default) renders in the secondary colour — the case-study results pattern.
- One hero number per slide; don't crowd a visual with stats.
