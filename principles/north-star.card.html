The value is in the principles, not the brand values. A token names a role and a function — never a concrete value.

1
Simple

Translate something complex, simply. Basic shapes, simple diagrams, one thing pulled from the post. “Good and tidy” beats “impressive.”

2
Recognisable

Consistency is the brand signal. Same header, footer, fonts and colour roles across a series. Strong makers don’t vary.

3
Save-trigger

Every visual earns a save by carrying a learning. The visual carries the save — not the caption.
