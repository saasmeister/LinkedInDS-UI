1

Post-first. The visual follows from the post or idea — never the reverse. If the post is missing, ask for it.

2

Pin the type, don’t guess. Single / carousel / infographic / quote is often borderline — ask how the user pictures it.

3

Offer pushback. “A funnel doesn’t fit — there are no sequential steps.” “This breaks the visual unity of your series.”

4

Build last — and always in threes. Only build once the approach is clear, then show three distinct variants. The user picks one, you iterate and show three again; keep the rest. Consistency within a series beats loose creativity.
