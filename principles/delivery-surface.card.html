Add to design system

Every visual is delivered on a canvas you can scroll through like a reel — click one to bring it to the hero, then export it as a PNG or a standalone HTML file. When a client approves a visual, Add to design system records it: approved variants are the queue the system learns from, and recurring patterns get promoted into real components (e.g. the browser-window “catalog of homepages” became the BrowserMock component).
