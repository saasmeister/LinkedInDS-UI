The test: if someone could say "AI made that" without doubt, it failed. Every variant runs this pass before it's shown. A deliberately invoked style pack is voice, not slop — its signature isn't flagged; the never-exempt checks still hold.

Refuse

  • Hero-metric template — big number + tiny label + 3 stats + gradient
  • Eyebrow on every variant by reflex; 01/02/03 as decoration
  • Side-stripe cards & identical/nested card grids
  • Gradient text, purple→blue AI-gradient, default glassmorphism
  • Three tints of one layout across A/B/C

Never exempt

  • Readable contrast — no grey-on-tint, no grey-on-colour
  • No default-font headline (Inter/system with no intent)
  • Real type hierarchy; nothing below ~24px
  • A/B/C genuinely differ — vary the mechanism, not the tint
  • Holds even inside a style pack
